AI in Nursing Education: The CIDI Revolution

How ChatGPT integration transformed critical thinking, problem-solving, and engagement in healthcare education

Based on research by Chang, C.-Y., Yang, C.-L., Jen, H.-J., Ogata, H., & Hwang, G.-H.
Educational Technology & Society, 2024

Traditional nursing education has long relied on lecture-based knowledge transfer, often lacking the interaction, practical drills, and personalized feedback needed to develop essential clinical reasoning skills.

A recent study integrated ChatGPT with the CIDI problem-solving model, creating a revolutionary approach that significantly improved learning outcomes. This interactive visualization explores their findings.

The Traditional Education Challenge

Nursing education has primarily relied on lectures with limited interaction, drills, or personalized feedback. Students often become passive recipients rather than active participants in their learning.

The CIDI Framework

Researchers integrated ChatGPT with Alex Osborn's CIDI model (Clarify, Ideate, Develop, Implement), creating a structured approach to problem-solving in nursing education.

Experimental Design

50 nursing students were divided into two groups. The experimental group used the CIDI-ChatGPT system, while the control group received traditional lecture-based instruction.

Significant Improvements

The CIDI-ChatGPT group achieved a 22% improvement in critical thinking, with a large effect size (η² = .414) and high statistical significance (p < .001).

Clarify

Students identified and defined the problem scope, establishing clear learning objectives and understanding constraints.

Ideate

The creative brainstorming phase where students generated diverse solutions through AI-guided exploration.

Develop

Ideas were evaluated and refined into concrete, feasible solutions through iterative AI conversations.

Implement

Students tested and adjusted their solutions, receiving real-time feedback from the AI system.

Problem-Solving Enhancement

The interactive nature of the CIDI-ChatGPT system allowed students to practice clinical reasoning in a safe environment, receiving immediate feedback on their approaches.

Measurable Results

Students using the AI system achieved a 27% improvement in problem-solving, with a substantial effect size (η² = .343).

Practical Application

Students designed a dietary education sheet for pregnant women, applying knowledge through the structured CIDI framework with AI guidance.

Engagement Matters

Beyond measurable skills, student engagement and enjoyment significantly impact knowledge retention and long-term application of learning.

Dramatic Difference

The CIDI-ChatGPT approach led to a 30% increase in learning enjoyment, suggesting students found the interactive AI experience more engaging.

Future Implications

These findings suggest that AI integration could revolutionize not just nursing education but many fields requiring complex problem-solving and critical thinking.

Transforming Education Through AI Integration

The research demonstrates that thoughtfully integrated AI systems can dramatically enhance educational outcomes. The CIDI model combined with ChatGPT created a learning environment where nursing students could safely explore, make mistakes, and develop the clinical reasoning skills essential for modern healthcare.

These improvements weren't marginal—they represent a fundamental shift in how students engage with complex healthcare concepts. As AI technology continues to evolve, its potential to transform education across disciplines becomes increasingly clear.

Rather than replacing educators, AI serves as a powerful tool to augment their ability to develop the next generation of healthcare professionals with the cognitive skills needed for an increasingly complex medical landscape.
